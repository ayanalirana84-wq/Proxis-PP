Proxis Privacy Policy

Creator: RonzDavil

Overview

Welcome to Proxis, your intelligent AI companion built for the new era of human–machine interaction. Privacy is not just a policy — it's our principle. Proxis is designed to protect your data and digital identity while delivering next-generation AI experiences.

1. Data Collection Philosophy

Proxis operates with a zero-tracking core. We don’t harvest, analyze, or sell your data. Every message you send stays private — processed only for generating responses, not for storage or profiling.

No personal data collection.
No analytics or tracking scripts.
No background data harvesting.

Your data belongs to you, not the system.

2. Conversations and Processing

Your chats with Proxis are handled securely. Conversations are processed in real time to craft intelligent replies and are never stored permanently. Once the chat ends, no records remain.

3. Third-Party Access

Proxis runs independently and does not integrate ads, trackers, or third-party SDKs. If future versions connect to verified AI services, all communication will remain encrypted and limited strictly to generating your requested responses.

4. Device Permissions

Proxis may request basic permissions (such as internet access) to enable AI functionality. It never accesses contacts, media, or location data.

5. Your Control

You’re always in control. You can clear all chats anytime, and uninstalling the app erases all locally stored data instantly.

6. Security

Proxis runs in a protected environment built to prevent leaks and unauthorized access. Every update is crafted with encryption and modern security in mind.
